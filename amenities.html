Bed And Baths
Three bedroom, three bathroom home which sleeps 12 comfortably. One upper master with a five piece ensuite and private balcony. Main floor master with a four piece ensuite with access to the back deck and hot tub. Both masters have a beautiful view of Lake Travis.
